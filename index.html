Inactive Moves

Processing an Inactive Move

Members seeking an Enroller/Sponsor change after inactivity must email europeplacements@doterra.com from their registered email address using this format:

  • • Wellness Advocate to be moved: (name and ID number)
  • • New Enroller: (insert "none" if no change)
  • • New Sponsor: (insert "none" if no change)

Requests must be submitted personally by the account holder from their registered email.

Period of Inactivity Required

  • • 6 months for Premier rank or below
  • • 12 months for Silver rank and above

Activity Definition

Activity includes: direct account transactions, product purchases, points orders, enrolling new members, earning commissions, or company/product sharing.

Important Notes

  • • A "Voluntary Termination Form" is recommended during inactivity to prevent unintended activity
  • • When an inactive move is requested, the Enroller has until the 10th of the following month to request a Sponsor change
  • • Note: Information provided here summarizes policy. The Wholesale Customer Agreement, Wellness Advocate Agreement, and doTERRA Policy Manual are controlling documents.
